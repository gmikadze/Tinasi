#TINASI WINERY

About Us

It all starts with planting and harvesting the finest quality Georgian grapes in Kvareli valleys, and in Mukuzani along the Alazani river. When grapes are ready, we harvest by hand. Then, we use machinery produced by the leading companies worldwide for all the processes that grapes demand, starting from grape preparation to fermentation and storage. Finally, after merging modern technologies and ancient traditions of winemaking, we preserves an old, unique taste of wine, create its own unrepeatable style and offer finest Georgian wines to the worldwide
